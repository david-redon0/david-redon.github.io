About

I am currently a PhD student, since September 2021, at Université de Lille (CRIStAL) within the CFHP and BONUS teams.

My thesis focuses on black-box function optimization within the framework of high-performance computing, especially for large-scale computing servers, under the direction of Pierre Fortin and Bilel Derbel.

I obtained a Master's degree in Computer Science from Université de Lille in 2021, in the "Complex Models, Algorithms and Data" (MOCAD) track.

Domains

My current research focuses on Function Optimization, especially Blackbox Optimization techniques, both Local and Global ; as well as on High-Performance Computing, working with both Shared and Distributed Memory architectures, especially for large-scale computing environments.

I am also highly familiar with Functional Programming.
